# Electricity

## Sector Overview

Overhauling electricity is a critical step, and tends to be the primary climate tech focus.

<Admonition title="Critical Goal" type="important">
  Reach 50% zero-emissions electricity by 2025 and 90% by 2035.

  Use the [Speed & Scale news tracking tool](https://speedandscale.com/okrs/2-0-decarbonize-the-grid/2-1-zero-emissions/) to monitor progress
</Admonition>

* The permitting process for new infrastructure
* Responding to peak demand
* The economics of buying and selling clean energy
